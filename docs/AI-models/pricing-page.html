❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after my free trial ends?
Your account remains active but you must upgrade to a paid plan to continue processing audio. Your data is preserved for 30 days.
Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades are prorated to your next billing cycle.
What counts as "1 AI use"?
Running AI duplicate detection, transcription, or PDF comparison on one audio file counts as 1 AI use (approximately £0.60-£1.20 depending on length).
Do unused AI credits roll over?
No, AI budgets reset monthly. However, Enterprise plans can request custom rollover options.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Cancel anytime with one click. No cancellation fees. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
